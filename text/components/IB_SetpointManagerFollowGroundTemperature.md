## IB_SetpointManagerFollowGroundTemperature

![](../../images/components/IB_SetpointManagerFollowGroundTemperature.png)


This setpoint manager is used to place a temperature setpoint on a system node that is derived from a current ground temperature. The ground temperatures are specified in different Site:GroundTemperature:* objects and used during the simulation.
